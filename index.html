About the Firm

Over Two Decades of
Distinguished Practice

Gacau Kariuki & Co. Advocates is a Nairobi-based law firm founded and led by Edward Gacau Kariuki — Advocate of the High Court of Kenya with over 25 years of experience at the bar.

"The law is not merely a profession — it is a responsibility. Every client who walks through our door deserves counsel that is both rigorous and deeply humane."

The firm has built its reputation on meticulous legal work, unwavering integrity, and genuine commitment to client outcomes. From high-stakes litigation to sensitive estate matters, Gacau Kariuki & Co. brings the same standard of excellence to every case.
